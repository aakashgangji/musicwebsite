About Me

I’m Aakash Gangji, an artist born on July 4, 2000, in Kalaburagi, Karnataka, India. My musical journey kicked off in 2019 when I released “The RCB Song,” a Kannada rap parody that quickly went viral and let me share my creativity and love for music with a wider audience. That moment marked the true beginning of my passion for making music that connects with people emotionally.

Since then, my sound has evolved. My debut single, “Thoda Karlo Mujhse Pyaar” (featuring Syed Mavahid & Abhishek Kanti, produced by Saikumar Hodalur), represents a new chapter in my artistry. This track is especially close to my heart—it’s raw, honest, and packed with emotion, blending heartfelt lyrics with contemporary melodies and production. Through every release, I strive to capture real feelings and tell stories that resonate with listeners.
